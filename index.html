02
StuffPro Pitcher Dashboard
Streamlit app built on your proprietary pitch-evaluation ML model: your strongest quantitative tool for pitcher evaluation. Trained on 3.6M+ college pitches, StuffPro achieves the following: 0.785 year-to-year stability (compared to 0.194 baseline), demonstrating exceptional reliability; 0.251 predictiveness predicting next-year observed run value at the pitcher level, a 30% improvement over the inherent volatility of actual performance; same-season correlation with run value: 0.283 for fastballs, 0.235 for sliders/curveballs, 0.152 for changeups. Pulls pre-computed StuffPro/PitchPro scores from cache parquets, visualizes movement/location/velocity via Trackman CSVs with per-pitch radial charts and performance distributions (light/dark theme support). This model cuts through the noise of single-season outcomes to identify sustainable pitcher skill and is essential for evaluating portal prospects with repeatable stuff.
